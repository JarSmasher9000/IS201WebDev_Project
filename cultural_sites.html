Deep Culture, Deep Roots Japanese Cultural Sites

On this page, you can find our highly recommended cultural sites to visit while in Japan. Japan has lots of deep history and with it comes lots of amazing, beautiful places to go and see!

Kyoto Area's Fushimi Inari

Just southeast of the heart of Kyoto lies a hidden gem in mount Fushimi. This cultural site offers scenic views of the major Kyoto area while walking under Japanese famous Torii gates. These Torii gates here at Fushimi Inari are particularly famous for their numerous and close together construction and layout creating a tunnel like path of gates leading the way to the summit of the mountain!

Although the summit is breathtaking, don't leave before checking out the shrine at the base of the mountain before the trailhead! Here you can partake in the aesthetic vibe of Japanese cultural sites via a world famous Shinto Shrine. Adjacent to the Fushimi Inari Shrine are many unique stores and restraunts to eat and dine at!
